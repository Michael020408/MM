Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MoodMap, the Emotion Combiner, and more.

How does the Emotion Combiner work? +

The Emotion Combiner blends two or three base emotions to generate a resulting emotion with its name, purpose, urge, and opposite. Simply select your emotions and click “Combine.”

Can I select the same emotion twice? +

No. Each emotion must be unique for a valid combination. The Combiner removes duplicates automatically.

What are base emotions? +

Base emotions are the eight fundamental feelings: Joy, Trust, Fear, Sadness, Disgust, Anger, Surprise, and Anticipation.

How can I interpret the results? +

Each resulting emotion includes its purpose (why you feel it), the urge (what it prompts you to do), and the opposite (a contrast to consider).
